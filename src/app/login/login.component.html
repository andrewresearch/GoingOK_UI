GoingOK uses Google login to authenticate you as a user.

GoingOK doesn't store any passwords or personal information. GoingOK only stores your Google email address for the purposes of sending you reminders and notifcations.

All of your reflections are stored separately and are not connected with your email address or any of your Google information. GoingOK does not provide any information to Google.

To login, click the "Sign in with Google button".
